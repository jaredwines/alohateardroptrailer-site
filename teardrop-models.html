Our Models

  • The Vaga

    The Vaga is your classic teardrop shape, ready for any adventure. Built on a 5’ x 8’ steel frame, this trailer can take you wherever you wander. Lightweight, corrosion-resistant aluminum creates a durable exterior. With customizable interior cabinets and a full galley, this teardrop comes fully equipped. With the Vaga, you no longer have to forfeit comfort to get to your off-road destination.

    Start My Order

  • The Renegade

    The Renegade is the perfect, most affordable trailer for the Weekend Warrior or anyone looking to get into nature without all the fluff. We now offer two versions of this model. A budget-friendly model, the Renegade LT is a no-frills, lightweight option that can be towed by any vehicle with a hitch. The Renegade AT model is the same blueprint as the LT, includes a 12-volt system, and sits on a heavy-duty chassis.
