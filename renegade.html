The Renegade

 

The Renegade Series

The Renegade is the perfect, most affordable trailer for the Weekend Warrior or anyone looking to get into nature without all the fluff. We now offer two versions of this model. A budget friendly model starting at less than $9,000, the Renegade LT is a no frills, lightweight option that can be towed by any vehicle with a hitch. The Renegade AT model is the same blueprint as the LT, includes the 12 volt system and sits on a heavy duty chassis. Upgrades to the chassis are available for the AT to allow for extreme off-road conditions.

Renegade LT: $8,950

The Renegade LT (Light-Terrain) model offers the basic essentials to get you off the grid and never have to worry about packing up a wet tent again. With a dry weight of only 800 lbs, it can be towed by virtually any vehicle.

  • 5’ x 8’ Light Weight Trailer Frame

  • One 26”x36” passenger-side door with vented window

  • 1750 lb Axle

  • 15” wheels, Tires 205/75R15

  • Interior Shelf with Storage Cubbies

  • Interior Side Shelves

  • LED Battery-powered lights

  • Insulated Construction

  • 4-Speed MaxxFan Vent Fan

  • Dry Weight is roughly 800 lbs

  • Aluminum color and shade of your choice (full color chart sent with estimate)

Renegade AT: $12,950

The Renegade AT (All-Terrain) model offers the essentials to get you off the grid and then some. The AT model sits on a heavy-duty 2x2 steel tubing allowing for greater durability on the backroads. This heavy-duty frame allows for more upgrades to get you even further off the grid. Additionally, the AT model comes fully wired with LEDs and charging ports inside and in the galley.

  • 5’ x 8’ Heavy Duty Trailer Frame

  • 2000 lb Torsion Axle

  • 15” wheels, Tires 205/75R15

  • Two 26”x36” doors with vented windows

  • Fully wired 12-volt electric system

  • Interior Shelf with Storage Cubbies

  • LED Reading lights

  • Galley Cabinets with side tables

  • LED Lights in galley hatch

  • LED Porch lights

  • Interior Side Shelves

  • LED Battery-powered lights

  • Insulated Construction

  • 4-Speed MaxxFan Vent Fan

  • Dry Weight is roughly 1100 lbs

  • Aluminum color and shade of your choice (full color chart sent with estimate)
