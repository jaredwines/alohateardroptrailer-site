The Vaga

 

The Vaga Series

The Vaga is the classic teardrop shape everyone loves with all the essentials you need nether you’re headed out for the weekend or taking a few months to travel cross-country. Enjoy the simplicity of camping light, combined with the comforts of a queen mattress inside your dream trailer.

Customize your cabin with locking cabinets, a large storage shelf, or a removable cot to fit your family’s needs. The full galley offers cooler and water slides, above and below counter storage, and detachable side tables to increase your cooking space.

Starting at: $13,950

  • 5’ x 8’ Trailer Frame

  • 3500lb Dexter Axle

  • 15” wheels, Tires 205/75R15

  • Side-mounted tables

  • Tri-folding Queen size mattress (78” x 58”)

  • Interior headboard and foot cabinets

  • Driver and passenger side doors with window

  • LED Reading Lights

  • One 12 volt socket in both cabin and galley

  • One dual-port USB outlet in both cabin and galley

  • 30 amp shore power inlet

  • Insulated Construction

  • 100Ah AGM Deep Cycle Battery

  • 4-Speed MaxxFan Vent Fan

  • Dry Weight is roughly 1200 lbs

  • Aluminum color and shade of your choice (full color chart sent with estimate)
