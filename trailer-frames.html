Trailer Frames

For your DIY Teardrop Trailer

One of the most important parts of your teardrop trailer build is the frame. You want a strong foundation that can take you wherever you want to wander. Unfortunately, finding a quality trailer frame can also be one of the hardest aspects of the build process.

Upon popular request, we now offer steel trailer frames!

Not only will this guarantee a strong frame for you to start your build, but it comes with a VIN, Bill of Sale, and Certificate of Origin which will make your life much easier when it comes time to register your teardrop.

Standard Frames Starting at $1,780

  • 5’x8’ Chassis made from 2” square tubing

  • 3500lb Axle

  • 15” Wheels

  • Radial Tires: 205/75R15

  • 2” coupler

  • VIN

  • Manufacturer’s Certificate of Origin (the first title)

  • EASY REGISTRATION!
